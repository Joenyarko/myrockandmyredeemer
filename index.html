Who We Are

My Rock And My Redeemer Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to creating sustainable change in underprivileged communities, with a special focus on single parents and orphans.

Who We Are and What We Stand For!

At MY ROCK AND MY REDEEMER FOUNDATION, we stand firm in our commitment to uplift and support the needy, underprivileged, physically challenged, and children with special needs in our society.

Through compassion, resources, and collective action, we strive to create pathways out of poverty, promote equality, and ignite lasting change.

Our Objectives

Our mission is to empower deprived communities through multifaceted support.

Support for Single Parents

We travel to rural areas to identify and support single parents who are struggling to provide for their families.

Care for the Elderly

Providing essential support, companionship, and healthcare assistance to the elderly, ensuring they live with dignity and love.

Care for Orphans

Taking care of neglected children on the streets and making regular donations to orphanages to ensure no child is left behind.

Clean Water Projects

Drilling boreholes in water-deprived areas and communities struggling with contaminated water sources.

School for the Blind

Providing specialized support, resources, and care for schools dedicated to the blind and visually impaired.
