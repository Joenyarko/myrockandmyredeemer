Who We Are and What We Stand For!

At MY ROCK AND MY REDEEMER, we stand firm in our commitment to uplift and support the needy, underprivileged, physically challenged, and children with special needs in our society.

Through compassion, resources, and collective action, we strive to create pathways out of poverty, promote equality, and ignite lasting change.
